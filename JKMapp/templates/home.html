Our Expertise

Thrilling Rides

Brace yourself for an adrenaline-pumping experience on our exciting rides! From heart-stopping roller coasters to whimsical carousels, we have something for everyone, ensuring that the whole family, friends, and couples alike can share in the excitement.

Delicious Dining

Take a break from the thrills and satisfy your taste buds with our diverse array of delectable foods. From mouth-watering cotton candy to savory treats, our food offerings are a culinary adventure waiting to be explored. There's something to please every palate!

Retail Therapy

Indulge in some retail therapy at our charming shopping stalls. Whether you're looking for souvenirs, trendy fashion, or unique gifts, our shops have a little bit of everything. It's the perfect way to commemorate your visit or find that special something.

Family, Friends, and Couples

Our Amusement Park is designed to create lasting memories for everyone. Whether you're here with family, friends, or a special someone, our enchanting atmosphere and diverse attractions cater to all age groups. It's the ideal place for quality time, laughter, and shared adventures.

About Us

Welcome to JKM entertainment, where enchantment meets excitement! As a premier Amusement Park, we pride ourselves on creating magical moments for visitors of all ages. Nestled in the heart of Gwalior, our park is a kaleidoscope of thrilling rides, delectable dining, and charming shopping experiences. From families seeking quality time to friends looking for adventure and couples in search of romance, we offer a diverse array of attractions to cater to everyone. With a commitment to safety and a passion for fun, JKM mela is not just a destination; it's a haven for laughter, joy, and unforgettable memories. Come join us on a journey where the ordinary transforms into the extraordinary!

Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of our live entertainment! From mesmerizing performances to captivating shows, there's always something magical happening at our Amusement Park. Check our event calendar for the latest lineup and make sure you don't miss out on the spectacular moments.
